In EPS, there are 4 class periods per day, each being 70 minutes long. However, every day one class period is a free period. Free periods give you extra time to socialize, finish homework, study or just relax and overall I love the freedom. Also, the teachers are really nice and helpfull which makes classes a lot more interesting and enjoyable. However, my favorite aspect of EPS is probably being able to see and talk to my friends.
