We use a unique curriculum which is tailored specifically for each and every student. Based on their learning style, we write songs to match their individual musical interest. We make our lessons fun and enjoyable for our students by incorporating ear training, composition, and teaching pieces they enjoy such as songs from the radio and movie soundtracks. Our goal is to make our students develop a life-lasting array of skills and passion for music, rather than just another dropped hobby.

From 2015, our students have been dominating all competitions entered, especially the National PTA Reflections competition. In the 2018-2019 school year, there were over 18,000 students entered in Washington State, 23 of them consisting of our students. A total of 9 students were awarded at the state level, 6 of them being from our initial 23 students. We won 66% of the state winners, when our students only attributed 0.13% of the total entrees! Our students are ranked 1st in Washington and 2nd nationally in composition!

While the average length required to receive a Diploma in Social Music (concert level pianist) from the American College of Musicians is 11 years, our students take an average of 4 years. Not only are they ranked 1st in Washington in student acceleration, they have received the highest grades averaging 9.71 out of 10 over the past 7 years.
