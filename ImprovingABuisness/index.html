WHAT IT TAKES TO PLAY THE PIANO:

An appreciation for music and basic classical education are key ingredients to musical success. At PayamsMusic, we promise to give you a learning experience that will develop your musical talents more than you have ever dreamed.

At PayamsMusic, we can provide you with anything from the basics of piano knowledge and playing by ear, to learning the pieces you enjoy. More advanced students can even learn compositional techniques and improvisation.
